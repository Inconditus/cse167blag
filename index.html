Post 3: Final status report

06/06/16

Lots of progress is being made as we near the final hours for our project deadline. We've implemented full arrow key movement for our tank, as well as WASD key movement for our camera. We've also created procedurally-modeled buildings, that change based off of psuedo-random seed.

We've also implemented bounding boxes and bullets and sound effects. Just have a few more features to implement before we're done.
